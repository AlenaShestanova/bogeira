N&S - natural and stainless - are jewelry pieces narrating contrast of future. This is the world full of the items, which combine softness of shape with hardness of material, rough surface and high tenacity with durability, streamline form and movability with dimensional and corrosion stability. This is the place where a human being is surrounded by personalized and human-friendly goods. Rings “N&S” are made of the material which possess above-listed qualities.
