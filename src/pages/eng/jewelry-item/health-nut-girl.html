Health nut girl (doing a bridge stretch)
Copper, patina, 2019

The health nut girl is a collective image of a Saint-Petersburg’s historic downtown citizen. There is the atmosphere of white nights, leaf bridges, soviet carpets, prerevolution parquet floor, morning yoga, coffee and sweets.
