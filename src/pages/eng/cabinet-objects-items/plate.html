РУС
Decorative plate
Ultima thule Brass,
silver-plating, gold-plating, patina, 2017

Waking dream of faraway and uncharted world. When I was a kid (and even now, at times), I read long science fiction books. Frequently they depictured other planets’ imaginary nature, which stood in stark contrast to ours, but inherently it was of similar structure. The brass decorative plate with split-level etching is an artistic vision of alien flora.
