Cassolette
Sepia Copper,
silver-plating, 2018

In Russian language the word “cuttle-fish” sounds like “karakatitsa”. Being informally a coarse word, it means somewhat awkward and clumsy. This goes against my awareness of this beautiful creature. Indeed, this is the most amazing and one of the smartest marine animals. I prefer to call it in the manner of the Latin language - sepia.
